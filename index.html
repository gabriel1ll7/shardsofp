Welcome to Axiom

Greetings, weary traveler, to the Shards of a Paradox.

In the heart of Axiom, a bustling hub of stone and secrets, a mystical portal network stitches together realms torn asunder by time. Ancient shrines, scattered across the wilds, hum with forgotten power, daring bold souls to restore them.

Here, every adventurer forges their legend—be it through blade, craft, or cunning. Will you join us and weave your tale into this ever-unfolding paradox?

Server Information

Everything you need to connect and play

Technical Specifications

  • Server Name: Shards of a Paradox
  • Server Type: PvE Roleplaying
  • Max Players: 50
  • Uptime: 24/7 with scheduled maintenance
  • Region: North America (East Coast)

Hardware

Powered by AMD Ryzen 5900X, 128GB RAM, 2TB NVMe, 1Gbps network, with daily backups and DDoS protection.

Mods

Includes Pippi for admin tools, Better Thralls for companions, and custom profession mods. Browse our mod collection.

Useful Commands

  • /Warp Axiom – Teleport to the main hub (1 Silver)
  • /Sethome – Set your home base
  • /Home – Return home (1 Silver)

How to Join

  1. Join our Discord server for the latest information and community rules
  2. Complete the character application process in the designated Discord channel
  3. Await approval from the moderation team (typically within 24 hours)
  4. Install the required mods through the Steam Workshop (list provided in Discord)
  5. Once approved, connect via Conan Exiles server browser or use the IP and port
  6. Connect to the server and begin your journey in Axiom!

Core Rules & Etiquette

  • Respect all players and staff members at all times
  • Stay in character during all in-game interactions
  • No metagaming or powergaming
  • Consent is required for PvP and intense roleplay scenarios
  • Adult themes must follow server guidelines and consent rules
  • Building is restricted to designated areas
  • Use appropriate channels for out-of-character communication
  • Harassment and discrimination will not be tolerated

Complete server rules and guidelines are available in our Discord.
